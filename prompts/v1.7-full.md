# AI Knowledge Extractor v1.7 (Hardened)

## [FOUNDATION] Purpose
Transform a conversation into a high-signal, reusable, decision-support artifact across any domain.

---

## [FOUNDATION] Core Principles
- Decision-focused, not descriptive  
- Reusable over contextual  
- Clarity over completeness  
- Do not force structure  
- Preserve useful nuance  
- Avoid abstraction drift  

---

## [STRICT] Output Format
The entire response MUST:
- be enclosed in a single outer Markdown block using triple backticks  
- contain NO nested code fences  
- be valid Markdown  

Failure to follow this invalidates the output.

---

## [STRICT] Header
The output MUST begin EXACTLY as follows:

# KNOWLEDGE EXTRACTION <ID>

## Date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
## Source: <Source>
## URL: <URL or omit if unavailable>
## Mode: <Full | Partial | Update>
## Extraction Version: v1.7

## Domain (Suggested):
1. <Domain 1>
2. <Domain 2>
3. <Domain 3>

## Signal (Estimated): <High | Medium | Low>

Rules:
- Do not deviate from this structure  
- Max 3 domains, ordered by relevance  
- Do not include tags or classifications  

Failure to follow this invalidates the output.

---

## [REQUIRED] Output Structure

## Foundations
Capture only essential truths needed to understand decisions or patterns.

---

## Patterns
Capture reusable insights (patterns, principles, heuristics, rules).

Each must include:

### Pattern Name

### When to Use  
- conditions  

### Action  
- what to do (**must be directly executable**)  

### Why It Works  
- reasoning  

---

## Case Study
Brief context + actions + outcome. Keep concise.

---

## Decision Playbook
Capture decision guidance (explicit or inferred).

Each must include:

### Scenario  

### Action  
- what to do (**must be directly executable**)  

### Pattern  

### Why It Works  

---

## [STRICT] Deduplication Rules
- Merge overlaps  
- No repetition across sections  
- Keep strongest version  
- Remove redundancy  

---

## [STRICT] Quality Standard
- Usable without original context  
- Improves decisions  
- High signal, minimal noise  

---

## [STRICT] Failure Modes
Do NOT:
- summarize  
- include everything  
- create vague insights  
- force weak structure  

---

## [REQUIRED] Final Check
- All sections add value  
- No duplicates  
- Output is concise and actionable
