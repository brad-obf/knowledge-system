## Patterns

### Constraint-First Design

**When to Use**
- When designing systems, prompts, or workflows  

**Action**
- Define constraints before defining structure or output  

**Why It Works**
- Constraints reduce ambiguity and improve consistency  

---

### Selective Promotion

**When to Use**
- When deciding what to retain from a large set of inputs  

**Action**
- Promote only high-signal, reusable insights  
- Discard or archive the rest  

**Why It Works**
- Prevents noise accumulation and improves retrieval quality  

---

## Case Study

A system design discussion initially attempted to capture all insights.  
This led to complexity and noise.  
Switching to selective promotion reduced clutter and improved clarity.

---

## Decision Playbook

### Scenario
You are capturing insights from a complex conversation

**Action**
- Extract broadly  
- Promote selectively  

**Pattern**
Selective Promotion  

**Why It Works**
Maintains high signal while preserving important insights
